About Nina

Primary care the way

Nina Pliakos is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of experience in primary and preventive care. She founded Evexia to reclaim medicine from the assembly-line model — to offer something slower, more personal, and more effective.

Her practice is built on the belief that great healthcare starts with a provider who actually knows you. Not your chart. You.

Nina offers unhurried appointments, evidence-based care, and a genuine partnership with each patient — whether you're managing a chronic condition, optimizing your health, or just haven't seen a doctor in too long.
