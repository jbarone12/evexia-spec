Meet Nina

The provider
you've been looking for

Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of clinical experience. She started Evexia because she believes the patient-provider relationship is the foundation of real healthcare.

Nina spent years in high-volume healthcare settings watching something erode: the relationship between patient and provider. Fifteen-minute appointments. Rotating faces. Charts instead of conversations. She started Evexia to restore what medicine lost.

Her approach is simple — listen first, treat second. Every patient deserves a provider who knows their name, their story, and their goals. Not just their lab values.

Nina's clinical passion is prevention. She believes most chronic disease is not inevitable — it's the result of missed opportunity. At Evexia, that opportunity is never missed.
