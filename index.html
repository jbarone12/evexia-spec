Nina Pliakos, FNP-C

Meet Nina Pliakos, FNP-C

Nina is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with a deep commitment to preventive care and patient education. She completed her Master's degree in Nursing at Arizona State University and holds certifications in advanced cardiovascular assessment, hormone optimization, and functional medicine principles.

Clinical Background

With over a decade of experience in urgent care, emergency medicine, and primary care settings, Nina has developed a nuanced understanding of how lifestyle, stress, nutrition, and sleep impact long-term health. She's treated thousands of patients across diverse backgrounds and health conditions.

Treatment Philosophy

Nina believes that healing happens when patients feel heard, understood, and empowered. She combines rigorous medical science with compassionate listening, taking time to understand your unique health story before recommending any treatment path.

Personal Interests

Outside the clinic, Nina is a devoted runner, home chef, and advocate for holistic wellness. She volunteers with community health initiatives and mentors nursing students at ASU. She's fluent in English and Greek.

Your First Appointment

1

Welcome & Assessment

You'll complete a comprehensive health history form (digital or paper), then meet with Nina in a calm, unhurried setting. She'll ask detailed questions about your health goals, past medical history, family history, and lifestyle.

2

Physical Exam & Labs

A thorough physical examination tailored to your age, health status, and concerns. Baseline lab work (blood work, vitals) may be ordered to establish a complete picture of your current health.

3

Personalized Plan

Nina will develop a customized treatment and wellness plan based on her findings. You'll discuss any recommendations, medications, lifestyle changes, and preventive strategies in detail.

Insurance & Pricing

We accept most major insurance plans. If you're uninsured, we offer transparent, competitive cash pricing without surprise bills.

Accepted Plans: Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, United Healthcare, Cigna, Humana, and others.

Copay & Coinsurance: Standard office visit copay applies. Preventive care may be covered at 100% by your plan.

Cash Patients: $150-250 for initial visit, $100-150 for follow-ups. No surprise charges.

Questions about your coverage? Call us and we'll verify your benefits before your first visit.
