Meet Nina

Nina Pliakos, FNP-C, brings a deep commitment to preventive, relationship-centered care to Evexia Primary Care. With years of experience in primary care medicine, she understands that the best healthcare happens when provider and patient truly work together.

Education & Credentials

  • University of Iowa — B.S. Human Physiology
  • Millikin University — B.S.N. Nursing
  • University of Cincinnati — M.S. Family Nurse Practitioner
  • Certification: Family Nurse Practitioner-Certified (FNP-C)

Before founding Evexia, Nina worked in established primary care settings, where she developed her philosophy: real healing happens when a provider takes time to know their patients as whole people, not just conditions to manage.

Why Evexia?

Evexia was born from a simple belief: healthcare should be personalized, unhurried, and focused on helping you achieve true wellness. Nina wanted to create a practice where prevention is paramount, where your concerns are heard, and where you partner with someone who genuinely cares about your long-term health.

As a solo practice, Evexia allows Nina to dedicate the time and attention each patient deserves — no rushed visits, no assembly-line medicine. Just thoughtful, compassionate care.

Insurance & Cost Transparency

We Accept Most Insurance Plans

Evexia Primary Care accepts most major insurance plans, including Medicare and Medicaid. We're committed to transparent, straightforward billing so you always know what to expect.

Your insurance benefits vary based on your specific plan. We recommend calling us before your first visit so we can verify your coverage.

Self-Pay Options

If you prefer to pay out-of-pocket or have high-deductible coverage, we offer competitive self-pay rates and flexible payment plans. We believe good healthcare should be affordable.

What's Typically Covered

  • Preventive visits (wellness exams): Usually 100% covered with no copay under most insurance plans
  • Chronic disease management: Copay or coinsurance applies (varies by plan)
  • Lab work: May require separate copay; subject to deductible
  • Telehealth visits: Same coverage as in-person visits
  • Medications: Subject to your pharmacy benefit and formulary

Questions About Billing?

Call us at 602-782-5740 or email info@evexiaprimarycare.com. We're happy to discuss costs upfront so there are no surprises.
