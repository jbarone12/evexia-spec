Welcome

A State of Optimal Well-Being

"Evexia" means a state of optimal well-being — vitality of mind, body, and spirit.

Evexia Primary Care is more than a doctor's office. It's a space where healthcare feels approachable, collaborative, and deeply personal. Every visit is relaxed and conversational, grounded in evidence-based medicine.

Founded by board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner Nina Pliakos, we believe primary care should be proactive, not reactive. We focus on prevention, early detection, and building lasting relationships with our patients.

Your Provider

Healthcare That Feels Personal

Nina Pliakos, FNP-C
Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner • Founder

Nina founded Evexia Primary Care with a simple belief: primary care should feel approachable and collaborative. Her visits are relaxed and conversational, while grounded in evidence-based medicine.

With a warm bedside manner and genuine passion for patient care, Nina takes the time to listen, answer every question, and build a care plan that addresses your complete well-being.

Patients describe Nina as someone who truly makes you feel seen and supported from the moment you walk in.
