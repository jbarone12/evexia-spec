Our Approach

A different kind of primary care

We believe real health isn't about rushing through appointments or checking boxes. It's about building a partnership where your provider actually listens.

At Evexia, every visit is unhurried. Every concern matters. We focus on prevention first, catching issues early and giving you the tools to take control of your health.

"Evexia" is a Greek word meaning a state of wellbeing that comes from genuine health and vitality — not just the absence of illness.

Nina Pliakos

FNP-C, Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner
Meet Your Provider

The provider you've been looking for

Nina Pliakos is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of clinical experience. She spent years working in high-volume healthcare settings and realized something was missing: time with patients, real conversations, and a chance to actually prevent disease instead of just managing it.

She started Evexia because she believes every patient deserves a provider who truly knows them — their values, their health history, their goals. That's where real healing begins.

Nina is passionate about preventive medicine, women's health, and helping patients navigate the complexity of chronic disease. She brings a thoughtful, evidence-based approach to every conversation.
