A Different Kind of Primary Care

We believe that real health isn't about rushing through appointments or checking boxes. It's about building a partnership where your provider actually listens, asks the right questions, and helps you understand what's happening in your body.

At Evexia, we focus on prevention first. Rather than waiting for problems to become serious, we work together to catch issues early, optimize your wellness, and give you the tools to take control of your health.

Every visit is unhurried. Every concern matters. Because health is deeply personal.

Meet Your Provider

Nina Pliakos is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of clinical experience. She spent years working in high-volume healthcare settings and realized something was missing: time with patients, real conversations, and a chance to actually prevent disease instead of just managing it.

"I started Evexia because I believe every patient deserves a provider who truly knows them—their values, their health history, their goals. That's where real healing begins."

Nina is passionate about preventive medicine, women's health, and helping patients navigate the complexity of chronic disease. She brings a thoughtful, evidence-based approach to every conversation and believes in meeting her patients where they are.

What We Treat

Wellness Exams & Prevention

Annual physicals, preventive screenings, and health optimization to catch issues early and keep you thriving.

Chronic Disease Management

Personalized treatment plans for diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disease, and other ongoing health conditions.

Women's Health

Comprehensive care including hormone management, contraception, fertility discussions, and menopause support.

Mental Health Support

We recognize the mind-body connection and provide screening, counseling, and referrals for mental wellness.

Medical Weight Management

Evidence-based weight management without the gimmicks—addressing root causes and supporting sustainable health.

Telehealth Visits

Convenient virtual appointments for follow-ups, medication management, and routine care.

Lab Coordination

Complete lab work ordered at your visit, with clear explanations of what your results mean.

Preventive Medicine

Proactive strategies to reduce disease risk and optimize your health before problems develop.

Transparent Pricing & Insurance

Insurance Plans Accepted

We accept most major insurance plans including Arizona Blue Cross, Aetna, Cigna, United Healthcare, and others. We'll verify your coverage before your visit so there are no surprises.

We also participate in direct-pay networks and offer discounted rates for uninsured patients.

Self-Pay Patients

No insurance? No problem. Our self-pay rates are competitive and transparent. First visit: $200. Follow-ups: $125.

We believe high-quality healthcare should be accessible, so we offer flexible scheduling and are happy to work with you on a payment plan if needed.
