Our Approach

A different kind of primary care

We believe your health is not a problem to be solved. It's a relationship to be built. Evexia exists for patients who want more — more time, more clarity, more of a provider who truly knows them.

Every visit is unhurried. Every concern is taken seriously. We lead with prevention, speak plainly, and treat you as an intelligent adult who deserves to understand their own health.

We measure success not by the number of patients we see, but by the depth of care we provide to each one.

Meet Nina

The provider you've been looking for

Nina Pliakos is a Board-Certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of clinical experience. She spent years in high-volume healthcare settings watching something erode: the relationship between patient and provider. She started Evexia to restore it.

Her approach is simple — listen first, treat second. Every patient deserves a provider who knows their name, their story, and their goals. Not just their lab values.

Nina's clinical passion is prevention. She believes most chronic disease is not inevitable — it's the result of missed opportunity. At Evexia, that opportunity is never missed.
